Solar Cooking Demonstration--FREE!

Item# solar-cooking-demonstrationfree

Product Description

Where: Urban Solar Solutions, 4641 Tanglewood Drive, Edmonston (Hyattsville), MD 20781

When: Saturday, April 25, 2009, 2-4 **Rain Date May 16, 2009 **

Come learn about the technology involved in solar cooking, taste a solar prepared dish and see a solar oven in action. Hear also about the the charitable efforts of SHE-Solar Household Energy, Inc. A nonprofit organization, SHE designs solar ovens and provides them free fo charge to women and families in lesser developed countries in Latin America, the Caribbean and Africa.

On site will be solar ovens for sale, books, solar oven plans and more. Just what you need to prepare to compete in our upcoming solar cookoff!
