Green Job Training Programs

Coming May 1, 2011:

1) Green Jobs in the DMV An educational video series on green jobs, where to find them and how to get them for DC, MD and VA residents. This video is designed for the educational systems. May be shown in classes and in presentations. Developed, in part, with a grant from the District of Columbia.

On sale starting February 1, 2011

2) Green Jobs in the New Economy, a training program for job counselors and advisers coming in March. This 5 session program will give you the tools to help your students and advisees make it in the real world. Tailored to Washington, DC and its surrounding territory, we will detail the green jobs available, the training opportunities and funding sources to acquire these jobs and even best practices for applying for and getting them.
