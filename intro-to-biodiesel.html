Intro to Biodiesel

Item# intro-to-biodiesel
$30.00

Product Description

Where: Urban Solar Solutions, 4641 Tanglewood Drive, Edmonston, MD

When: Sunday, April 19, 2009, 2-4

Can you really grow cleaner fuel for your car? Do you have to rely on corn based ethanol, with all of its inefficiencies or is there another option?

Come learn about Biodiesel from the experts of Green Guild Biodiesel Coop of Mt. Ranier.

Adam Schwartz, co-founder of Green Guild, will come to tell all. A Maryland Grad, Adam has been actively building Green Guild from the ground up. Even going so far as to build their filling station from a shipping crate powered by solar panels! Very cool, he is open to questions about the building as well as about the Guild and biodiesel. Adam, Welcome!
